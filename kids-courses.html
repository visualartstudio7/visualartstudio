Course description

Students continue to build on skills learnt, elements and principles of art, critical and creative skills, art history and culture. They learn to work with various tools, techniques and mediums and incorporate them into their artworks. Students are encouraged to make choices that enhance the communication of their ideas. Emphasis is placed on the use of sketchbooks to explore ideas and experiment with mediums. Exposure to the works of famous artists and the arts and crafts of artisans from many cultures plays an integral role in the curriculum.

What you'll learn

  • New Batches: batches commence on January and July.
  • Class Frequency: Classes are held once a week.
  • Certificate: A certificate is awarded on completion of one year (January-December)
